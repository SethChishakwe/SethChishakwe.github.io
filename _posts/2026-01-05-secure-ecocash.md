## üö® The Reality: Common EcoCash Scams in Zimbabwe

Before we get to solutions, let's understand what we're up against:

1. **Phishing SMS/WhatsApp:** "Your EcoCash wallet is suspended. Send PIN to restore."
2. **Fake Customer Care:** Callers pretending to be from Econet asking for your PIN.
3. **SIM Swap Fraud:** Criminals getting a duplicate SIM to access your EcoCash.
4. **Shoulder Surfing:** People watching you enter your PIN at POS terminals.

## üõ°Ô∏è 3 Immediate Actions You Can Take TODAY

### 1. Create a STRONG, UNIQUE EcoCash PIN
**The Problem:** Research shows 30% of Zimbabweans use their phone unlock PIN for everything.

**The Solution:**
‚ùå DON'T USE:
1234 or 0000
Your birth year (1999, 2000)
Repeated digits (1111, 2222)
Phone unlock PIN

‚úÖ DO THIS:
Create a random 5-6 digit number
Mix it up: e.g., 48263 (not a pattern)
Change it every 3 months
Never write it down with your phone


**Pro Tip:** Use a "PIN sentence" method. Example: "My son Tawanda was born in 2020" = Mstwbi2020 = numbers from that = 420 (then add random digits).

### 2. NEVER Share Your PIN via SMS, Call, or WhatsApp
**The Scam Pattern:**
üì± SMS Example:
"Dear Customer, your EcoCash wallet has temporary restrictions.
Reply with PIN for verification. From: E-CONET Support"

üìû Call Example:
"Hello, I'm from Econet technical department. We're upgrading systems
and need your PIN to secure your account."

**How to Respond:**
- **LEGITIMATE COMPANIES NEVER ASK FOR YOUR PIN**
- Hang up immediately
- Save the number and report to Econet (111)
- Forward scam SMS to Econet's fraud department
- If concerned, visit an OFFICIAL Econet shop (don't call back!)

### 3. Enable ALL Security Features
**Most people only use basic EcoCash. Turn these ON:**

| Feature | How to Enable | Why It Helps |
|---------|--------------|-------------|
| **Transaction Alerts** | Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí ALL Alerts | Know immediately if unauthorized transaction occurs |
| **Daily Limit** | Set limit to your average daily spend | Limits damage if compromised |
| **Withdrawal PIN** | Different from main PIN for cash-out | Extra layer at POS/agents |
| **Biometric** | If phone supports fingerprint/face ID | Prevents unauthorized app access |

**To enable these:**
1. Dial `*150#`
2. Go to "My EcoCash" ‚Üí "Settings"
3. Enable everything in "Security Settings"

2. Did NOT reply
3. Reported to Econet via 111
4. Shared warning in family WhatsApp group
