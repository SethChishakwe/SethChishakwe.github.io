1. Create a STRONG, UNIQUE EcoCash PIN

The Problem: Research shows 30% of Zimbabweans use their phone unlock PIN for everything.

DON'T USE:

  • 1234 or 0000
  • Your birth year (1999, 2000)
  • Repeated digits (1111, 2222)
  • Phone unlock PIN

DO THIS:

  • Create a random 5-6 digit number
  • Mix it up: e.g., 48263 (not a pattern)
  • Change it every 3 months
  • Never write it down with your phone

Pro Tip: PIN Sentence Method

Example: "My son Tawanda was born in 2020" = Mstwbi2020 = numbers from that = 420 (then add random digits to make 5-6 digits).

1. Gadzira PIN yeEcoCash YAKASIMBA, YAKASIYANA

Dambudziko: Tsvagurudzo inoratidza kuti 30% yevaZimbabwe vanoshandisa PIN yavo yekuvhura foni pazvinhu zvese.

USA SHANDISE:

  • 1234 kana 0000
  • Gore rekuzvarwa rako (1999, 2000)
  • Manhamba akadzokororwa (1111, 2222)
  • PIN yekuvhura foni

ITA IZVI:

  • Gadzira nhamba isina kurongeka ine 5-6 manhamba
  • Sakanidza: semuenzaniso, 48263 (kwete patani)
  • Shandura pamwedzi mitatu
  • Usambofa wakanyora pasi nefoni yako

Zano rehunyanzvi: Nzira yePIN Sentence

Muenzaniso: "Mwanakomana wangu Tawanda akazvarwa muna 2020" = Mstwbi2020 = manhamba kubva ipapo = 420 (wozowedzera manhamba asina kurongeka kuti aite 5-6 manhamba).

1. Yenza i-PIN ye-EcoCash EQINILE, EYINGQAYIZIVELE

Inkinga: Ucwaningo lubonisa ukuthi ama-30% abaseZimbabwe asebenzisa i-PIN yabo yokuvula ifoni kuzo zonke izinto.

UNGAZE WASEBENZISE:

  • 1234 noma 0000
  • Unyaka wokuzalwa kwakho (1999, 2000)
  • Amadijithi aphindwe (1111, 2222)
  • I-PIN yokuvula ifoni

YENZA LOKHU:

  • Yenza inombolo engahleliwe enamadijithi ama-5-6
  • Yixube: isibonelo, 48263 (hhayi iphethini)
  • Yishintshe njalo ezinyangeni ezintathu
  • Ungaze wayibhala phansi nefoni yakho

Ithiphu Yobungcweti: Indlela ye-PIN Sentence

Isibonelo: "Indodana yami uTawanda wazalwa ngo-2020" = Mstwbi2020 = izinombolo ezivela lapho = 420 (bese ungeza amadijithi angahleliwe ukuze enze amadijithi ama-5-6).

2. NEVER Share Your PIN via SMS, Call, or WhatsApp

SMS Example:

"Dear Customer, your EcoCash wallet has temporary restrictions. Reply with PIN for verification. From: E-CONET Support"

Call Example:

"Hello, I'm from Econet technical department. We're upgrading systems and need your PIN to secure your account."

How to Respond:

  • LEGITIMATE COMPANIES NEVER ASK FOR YOUR PIN
  • Hang up immediately
  • Save the number and report to Econet (111)
  • Forward scam SMS to Econet's fraud department
  • If concerned, visit an OFFICIAL Econet shop (don't call back!)

2. USAMBOFA WAKAPA PIN YAKO neSMS, Runhare, kana WhatsApp

Muenzaniso weSMS:

"Dear Customer, wallet yako yeEcoCash ine zvirambidzo zvenguva pfupi. Reply nePIN yekuongorora. From: E-CONET Support"

Muenzaniso weRunhare:

"Hello, ndinobva kuEconet technical department. Tiri kuvandudza masystem uye tinoda PIN yako kuchengetedza account yako."

Maitiro Ekupindura:

  • MAKAMBANI ECHOKWADI HAAMBO KUMBIRA PIN YAKO
  • Dimbura runhare pakarepo
  • Chengetedza nhamba woita report kuEconet (111)
  • Forward scam SMS kubazi rehutsotsi reEconet
  • Kana uine hanya, enda kuEconet shop chaiyo (usafona zvakare!)

2. UNGAZE WANIKAZE I-PIN YAKHO nge-SMS, Ucingo, noma i-WhatsApp

Isibonelo se-SMS:

"Dear Customer, i-wallet yakho ye-EcoCash inemikhawulo yesikhashana. Phendula nge-PIN yokuqinisekisa. Kusuka: E-CONET Support"

  • Uma unokhathazeka, vakashele isitolo se-Econet ESISEMTHETHWENI (ungabuyi ucinge!)
